Terry wrote five books about my adventures as a witch - The Wee Free Men, A Hat Full of Sky, Wintersmith, I Shall Wear Midnight and The Shepherd's Crown. Looking back, it's hard to believe I was only nine years old in The Wee Free Men.

Really, most of most of my time as a witch is spent scrubbing floors, bandaging wounds, delivering babies, and the like. I just do the job that is in front of me.
